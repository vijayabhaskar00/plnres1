Our President

Message from P L Narsimha Reddy

Education not only prepares the youth to meet the challenges faced in the society but also helps them to improve their living standards.
Three decades of service in the government sector as a teacher and Headmaster, made me realize that many young girls and boys fail to attain higher education due to poverty. Believing education to be the most powerful weapon to change the world, we cannot go blind on such issues. Every young mind has the right to dream and achieve it. As responsible citizens we just have to extend some support that gives them courage and guidance in reaching their goals.
This thought has led the like-minded people join hands to form this Educational Society that identifies the needy and extends supports till they get settled in their life.
This spirit of togetherness and commitment has made the group raise year by year in reaching out to the needy. Its wonderful to see the youth, who received support from this society, joining as members after attaining jobs. Such sense of responsibility towards the society is what the team has dreamt in the initial stages. Hope to continue this spirit for ever with the support of all the esteemed members.
Thank you all for joining hands in the noble cause of educating the needy.

"Coming Together is Beginning.
Keeping Together is Progress.
Working Together is Success."
